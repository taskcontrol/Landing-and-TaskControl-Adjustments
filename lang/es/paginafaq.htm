How do 'Escrow' payments work?

 

By paying all or part of the agreed fees via an escrow account for the Consultant, the money is held securely and independently by goitglobal until all parties are satisfied that the Consultation has been completed, at which time the Buyer releases the funds. Should any dispute or problem arise, either goitglobal or the Consultant can release the funds back to the Buyer, and in the event of a disagreement goitglobal will mediate. By using escrow, the Consultant knows that funds have been commited for his benefit, but he/she doesn't have access to the money until the completion of the Consultation. So all parties to the agreement can be confident that payment will be made and the Consultation will be completed to everyone's satisfaction. The escrow payment functionality can be accessed via the "Manage My Account" section.
